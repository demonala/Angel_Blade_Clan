TENTANG KAMI

Beyond the mortal sphere of evade, where the hunted are devoured by unending night, there is forged a clan feared by foes and revered by whispers—Angels Blade.

We are no fleeting guild, nor the fragile gathering of wanderers. We are covenant, carved in silence and bound in blood, sworn upon the edge of eternity. Those who cross our threshold are no longer lone souls, but vessels of the blade’s undying flame, bermain evade bersama dan meningkatkan skill.

Within our fellowship, thy skill is honed upon the whetstone of peril, thy spirit tested amidst the chaos of pursuit, thy strength ascended beyond mortal measure. Here, laughter entwines with dread, and every fall is but the prelude to a greater rise.

Angels Blade is both sanctuary and abyss, a dominion where unity is tempered in darkness, where the bond of brethren outlasts the void itself. To join is to abandon the frailty of solitude, to surrender thy fear, and to embrace the eternal oath that binds us as one. Step forth, if thy courage dares defy the abyss. Yet heed this: once marked by the sigil of Angels Blade, there is no return. Let's join us, avatar doesn't matter, we accept every kind of person.
